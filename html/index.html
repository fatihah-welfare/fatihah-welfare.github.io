Our Mission

As local Muslim population is currently well on increase, the project is to establish a larger multi-functional Islamic facility capable of serving the spiritual and social needs of local Muslim community living in Hackney through prayer, education and community activities in English and Arabic language.

Fatihah Welfare Community has been striving for many years for a larger facility to cater for the needs of the local community.
